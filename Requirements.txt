Minimum System Requirements:

Operating System:

Windows 10 or later
macOS 10.14 (Mojave) or later
Linux (Ubuntu 18.04 or later)

Processor:

Dual-core processor (Intel Core i3 or equivalent)

RAM:

4 GB RAM

Storage:

At least 500 MB of free disk space for the application and dependencies
Additional space for storing images (depends on the number of users)

Graphics:

Integrated graphics (Intel HD Graphics or equivalent)

Camera:

A webcam or external camera compatible with the system

Software:

Python 3.6 or later
OpenCV (version 4.5 or later)
MySQL Server (version 5.7 or later)
Tkinter (comes pre-installed with Python)
CustomTkinter (latest version)
